## Conversion Funnel

### Stage 1: Awareness (Cold Outreach)
**Goal:** Get them to respond

**Tactics:**
- Pattern interrupt opener
- Personalized observation
- Quick value statement

**Example:**
> "Yo! Saw you crashed last week. That sucks. Quick question: you got backup? If not, I can help. 2 min convo?"

**Success metric:** 40% response rate

---

### Stage 2: Interest (First Conversation)
**Goal:** Qualify and educate

**Tactics:**
- Ask about their pain points
- Share relevant data
- Offer free trial

**Example:**
> "What's your biggest fear about crashes? [listen] Yeah, that's common. Here's how we solve it: [explain]. Want to try free for 7 days?"

**Success metric:** 60% trial signup

---

### Stage 3: Trial (7-Day Free)
**Goal:** Get them to complete first workout

**Tactics:**
- Onboarding checklist
- Daily check-ins
- Quick wins

**Example:**
> "Welcome! Here's your first workout: Morning Cardio. Takes 5 min. Do it now, I'll wait. [after completion] Nice! That's your first fitness point. 9 more for Bronze tier."

**Success metric:** 70% complete first workout

---

### Stage 4: Conversion (Trial â†’ Paid)
**Goal:** Convert to paid membership

**Tactics:**
- Show progress made
- Highlight member benefits
- Limited-time offer

**Example:**
> "You crushed 5 workouts this week. That's 50 fitness points. You're in the top 20%. Ready to go paid and unlock Silver benefits? First month is 50% off."

**Success metric:** 60% trial-to-paid

---

### Stage 5: Retention (Active Member)
**Goal:** Keep them engaged

**Tactics:**
- Weekly check-ins
- Celebrate milestones
- Community events

**Success metric:** 80% monthly retention
